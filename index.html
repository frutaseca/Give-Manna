🕊️✞Give Manna✞🕊️



🧔🏾🙌🏽🌤️"It’s something that began back in September of 1983 when the sitting mayor, Reggie Holtzendorf, approached a number of area ministers from across the community, including the Rev. Jim Crandall and the Rev. Buzz Yarborough. They collectively founded the mission, with the support of the city, as a way to aid the homeless and needy population in the downtown area. "🌤️🙌🏽🧔🏾





🍞Manna House is a soup kitchen located in Brunswick that is supported by volunteers, who serve thousands of free meals per month. St. James Lutheran Church members have committed themselves to serving at Manna House on the third tuesday of every month, and everyone is invited to join us in this fun and meaningful area of service.🍞



Volunteering Information

Volunteer hours with the St.James Lutheran Church are the third tuesday of every month from 11:30am - 1pm. You can find Manna House of Brunswick at 1408 G St. in Brunswick, Georgia 31520.
